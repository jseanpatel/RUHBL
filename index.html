Who we are:

The Redding United History Bowl League is an extension of the National History Bee and Bowl Competition which serves the greater Northern California. By uniting participating schools, the league has helped students practice and apply historical analysis and complex thinking skills.

Our goal:

As an student-run organization, we aim to advance the historical knowledge of students in the form of a fun, competitive enviorment. Students are benefitted in their respective Social ddScience classes while at the same time fostering a friendly rivalry between neighboring schools.

Our own history:

In the Winter of 2016, History Bowl first took root at Shasta High School. Later that year to meet the demand for increased, easily-accessible competition Shasta High Schools sister school, University Prepratory High School, also opened a team. Since then, similar programs have bene established at nearby Central Valley High School as well as West Valley High School.

What the competition is:

The Redding United History Bowl League is based on the competition format commonly found in association football (soccer). Through this structure, teams in the league with compete in three tournaments throughout the year, and their finals records are summed to determine finishing league placements. Point differentials will break ties.
